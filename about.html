Michael John Stevens goes by many names. Blizzerd, Blizz, Snowball, The Ice King. No matter which way we decide to put it, it does

not change who he is. A man. A man who has found love in art and culture. To say these things helped him grow would be an understatement.

He has such a passion for them, and he cannot stick to just one facet. He enjoys rapping and singing, as well as poetry. Words have been his saving grace many a times.

He also loves taking photos or films of the beautiful elements Mother Earth has to offer. I know you may be thinking, how do I know so much about Michael Stevens? Well, that's because I am Michael Stevens. So, head on over to the contact page and give me some views.
